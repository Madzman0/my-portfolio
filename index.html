About Me

I am a multi-skilled creative and tech-driven professional specializing in music production, web development, data analysis, and intelligent automation. I produce high-quality beats, engineer audio in FL Studio and Reaper, and design visually appealing content using Photoshop and Blender. On the tech side, I build modern, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind, and Node, with additional experience in backend tools like MySQL, Django, and PHP. I also work with Python and R for data preprocessing, analysis, and machine learning. Beyond development, I create automated digital systems—from forex chart-analysis bots to social media posting assistants—to help clients save time, grow their brand, and make smarter decisions. My mission is to combine creativity, technology, and strategy to deliver solutions that are modern, efficient, and impactful.
